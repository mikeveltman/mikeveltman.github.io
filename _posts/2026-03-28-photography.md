For the photos on this website, I'm using a FUJI X100VI as my main camera. I do not apply any postprocessing on my photos. In a rare case I apply cropping or alignment (some photos currently in some of the projects could use it, but I also like the imperfection it creates). The goal is to keep the picture the same as when they were taken. This forces me to get familiar with my camera, be better at taking photos without relying in patching things up in post.

The songs displated on the photo projects (these are often little trips or holidays as of now) try to add an extra dimension.

In the future, I might start adding my own music to the projects.

By the way, thanks for checking out my website.
